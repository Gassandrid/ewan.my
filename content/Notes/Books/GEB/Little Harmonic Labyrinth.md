The _Little Harmonic Labyrinth_ means several things in this context. On the one hand, it is a semi niche piece of music composed **Sebastian Bach**.

But in the context of [[Gödel, Escher, Bach]], it is a chapter dedicated to a dialogue between [[Achilles]] and [[The Tortoise]] about **recursion** and **self-reference**.

---

A personal favorite excerpt from this chapter was the dialogue involving the Genie and the idea of GOD. But in this case, GOD is actually [[God Over Djinn]], a recursive acronym serving as the mechanism for holy requests.

---

## Music

The musical piece itself is described in this chapter by how it leaves the reader/listener in a state of confusion, as it seems to be going in circles, but never quite returning to the starting point.

Just like how our logical brains are looking for a base case or a stopping point, the music seems to be trying to trick us into thinking it is just about to return to its starting point, but it never quite does.
