**"Cube with magic ribbons"** is a lithograph created by [[M.C. Escher]] in 1957. I encountered it in a dialogue between the [[The Tortoise]] and [[Achilles]] when reading [[Gödel, Escher, Bach]].

While the main theme from GEB about this piece was the sort of *suction cups* on the ribbons, the first thing I noticed was that the ribbons themselves were [[Mobius Strip]]s, but thanks to the interesting interlock between the two strips, I felt more like it was a sort of "Mobius Plane" if that makes any sense.
