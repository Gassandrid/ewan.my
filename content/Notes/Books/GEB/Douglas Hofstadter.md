[[Douglas Hofstadter]] is a Computer Scientist, PhD physicist, and Cognitive scientist. While his work in research of the mind and physics is impressive, he is best known for his book [[Gödel, Escher, Bach]].

His work in bringing together ideas of logic/[[Mathematics]] with the creative side of human intelligence has formed quite a complex and interesting theory of intelligence, for which I have found quite interesting in reading his book.
