isolated, closed trajectory in the phase space of a nonlinear [[Dynamical Systems Theory|dynamical system]]. self sustained oscillation.

Extension of a [[Fixed Point Classification|Fixed Point]] to the case of flow based systems. Another method you can do is to find the period, and take a snapshot at a given time point each period, you can convert the limit cycle into a fixed point.
