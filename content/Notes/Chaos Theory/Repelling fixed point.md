A fixed point $p$ is **repelling** (or a **source**) if $\exists \epsilon > 0 :$ all $x \in N_{\epsilon}(p)$ except $p$ eventually map outside of $N_{\epsilon}(p)$.

In plain English, nearby points move away from $p$ under iteration.
