Inosine Monophosphate

## Bug Observations

### Mix With Acetate

When mixed with the **acetate**, they appeared to slowly start to move to the new concentration. In addition, they also appeared to **diffuse** at a faster rate, moving both faster and switching direction more often.

### Mix with Potassium Chloride

They appear to move to the "bridge" between the two mixtures, but wont **cross** into the Potassium Chloride, seeing to desired the very edge of the mixutre.

After a while, they also appear to starting **spinning in place**, remaining on the bases' side.

### Mix with Quinine

Surface areas of both samples consolidated quite quickly, bugs appear to be moving much slower after. Later, all the bugs moved to the very edge of the now singular circular sample body.

### Mix With Glutamate

There was one that seemed to cross over, but besides that they all kind of swam away from the Glutamate.

After a while, they all have seemingly swum to the other side.
