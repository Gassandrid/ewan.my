## Formal Experiment Design

We define our trials as a set of all trials $n$, where $n\geq 10$:

$$
T=\{ T_{1},T_{2},\dots , T_{n} \} \in \mathbb{Z}^{\geq 10}
$$

Each trial is defined as a 5-tuple:

$$
T_{n}:= \left\{  P_{n}, P_{n}\in C, P_{n}\in T, \frac{P_{n} \in C}{P_{n}}, \frac{P_{n}\in T}{P_{n}}  \right\}
$$

**Where:**

- $P_{n}$ is the total # of Paramecia
- $P_{n}\in C$ is the total # of Paramecia in the Control
- $P_{n} \in T$ is the total # of Paramecia in the Test
- $\frac{P_{n}\in C}{P_{n}}$ is the *proportion* of Paramecia in the Control
- $\frac{P_{n}\in T}{P_{n}}$ is the *proportion* of Paramecia in the Test

**Constants**:

- Duration = $2.5$ minutes
- Distance = $\frac{1}{4}$ inch
- Count = $3$ drops
