***Enzymes** are [[Proteins]] that increase the rates of chemical reactions in [[Cell]]s.*

*Virtually ALL cellular reactions require an enzyme to occur **fast enough** to be useful (essential for life)*

---

## Chemical Reactions

Enzymes increase the rate of chemical reactions by **lowering the activation energy** requires for a reaction to occur.

![[enzymeActivationEnergy.png]]

---

## Components

- **Active Site**: the part of the enzyme where substrates interact/bind
	- this has a specific shape
	- and forms many [[Bonds#Non-Covalent Bonds|noncovalent]] bonds with substrates
