## Grade Schema

### Interactive Textbook Quizzes

There will be **14** of these, they are worth $20\%$ of the grade. They can be retaken, and should be considered like homework.

### In Class Activities

These are unannounced in class quizes that will happen once a week. like the textbook quizzes, there will be 14 of them.

They are worth $0-2$ points, where 1 is subpar and 2 is standard.

### iClicker

Standard attendance, several questions per class which are given credit apon submission, not answer choice. You must do at least $75\%$ for that day's credit.
