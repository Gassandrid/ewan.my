_Islam's holiest text._

Holy book for [[Muslim]], primary but not only sacred source of Islam. It is noted as literally "recited", in that it is [[Muhammad]]'s recitation of the literal word of God

It is important to note that unlike a lot of other religious texts, the Qur'an is interpreted to be the _actual_ word of God, not written with any human authorship. This is a key distinction in Islamic theology.

**Vocalized Arabic**: ٱلْقُرْآن‎
**Quranic Arabic**: ٱلۡقُرۡءَان‎
