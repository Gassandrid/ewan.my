**Muhammad** (570 - 632 CE) is considered the "last prophet" in Islam.

A _perfect_ human being, the ideal muslim, and the "seal of the prophets" - meaning no more prophets will come after him. Not God, not an angel, not a saint, not a mystic, not a guru.

At age 40, Muhammad began receiving revelations from God ([[Allah]]) while meditating, through the angel Gabriel (Jabreel).

---

## Role of Muhammad

_The_ example of good religious, ethical, and moral behavior for Muslims via:

### Hadith

Sayings of the Prophet Muhammad, "'Aisha reported that [[Muhammad]] said..."

**Crucial For**:

- ritual
- customs
- worldviews
- law ( sharia and fiqh )
- everything, basically

### Sunna

The implicit or explicit actions, beliefs, mannerisms exhibited by Muhammad. The Sunna is the second most important source of Islamic law after the Quran.

**Crucial For:**

- ritual
- customs
- worldviews
- behaviors, cultivated and desired
