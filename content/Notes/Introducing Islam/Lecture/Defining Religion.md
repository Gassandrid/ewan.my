## Lecture

>[!Question]
> Why Cant We Draw the Prophet Muhammed and His Companions?
> - This varies from Muslim to Muslim - some families and people actually do have pictures of the prophet, even if some others might view it as blasphemous.

>[!Success]- Answer
> It is partly to do with not seeing the prophet as a god himself - by drawing him you are breaking this. But this is not true for all practices of Islam, you will see many depicitions.
> - A part of this became a much bigger problem when the concept of the photograph came around, as this suddenly became something

>[!Question]
> Hijab vs Burka?

>[!Success]- Answer
> [[Hijab]] - a more loose term for covering between x and y, can be anything from a bandana in some cultures and a full covering in others
> [[Burka]] - a full body covering, not a loose term

## Readings

### Religion and Culture

As mentioned, when we talk about religion, we talk about religion **and** culture. The culture of a persons' society will always be strongly influenced by the predominant religion of that society.

This goes both ways - the culture and location a person practices their religion also influence them.
