## Reading from Farah Bakaari, "Islam and Its Others"

**Bakaari** asks us to think about who has the _right_ to claim the center or periphery of **Islam**, while seeding a lot of terms we'll get to know over the course.

**Our job** is to resist claiming either - to listen to what [[Muslim]]s tell us about what they do, why they do it, and how htey make sense of their sacred sources, lived lives, and internal/external debates.
