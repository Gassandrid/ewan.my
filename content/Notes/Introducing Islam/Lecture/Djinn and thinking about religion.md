*Why **jinn/djinn?** what do your think we gain by taking this as our content starting point? what can you imagine is the point?*

---

[[Djinn]] are known to be both malicious but also beneficent, depending on the specific [[Djinn]].

There are a lot of different kinds of Djinn to be aware of, some of the most common include [[Marid]], [[Ghul]], [[Ifrit]], and [[Si'lat]].

There exist places called [[Djinn Joint]]s, where Djinn are believe to gather "the djinn run this place". A specific example is [[Feroz Shah Kotla Fort]].
