The VTA is the origin of the mesolimbic and mesocortical dopamine pathways — the core of the brain's reward circuit. VTA dopamine neurons encode **reward prediction errors**: they fire when outcomes are better than expected and are suppressed when they're worse. This makes the VTA central to reinforcement learning in both biological and artificial frameworks.

Its projection to the [[Nucleus Accumbens]] (mesolimbic) drives motivation and habit, while its projection to the [[Prefrontal Cortex]] (mesocortical) modulates working memory and executive control.
