The midbrain is the most rostral brainstem segment, connecting the forebrain above to the pons and medulla below. It contains the [[Tegmentum]] (including [[Ventral Tegmental Area]] and [[Substantia Nigra]]) and the tectum (colliculi for visual/auditory reflexes).
