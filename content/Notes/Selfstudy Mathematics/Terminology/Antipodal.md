**Antipodal** points are pairs of points that are directly opposite each other on a sphere. In mathematical terms, if you have a point $x$ on an $n$-dimensional sphere $S^n$, its antipodal point is denoted as $-x$. The antipodal point is obtained by reflecting $x$ through the center of the sphere. Another word for these points is **diametrically opposite points**.
