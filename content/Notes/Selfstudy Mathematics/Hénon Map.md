Henon map is the classic example of a discrete time [[Dynamical Systems Theory|dynamical system]] in two dimensions. One of simplest 2d systems that exhibit [[Chaos|chaotic behavior]].

defined as taking point $(x_{n},y_{n})$ and mapping to new point:

$$
\begin{cases}
x_{n+1} = 1 - ax^{2}_{n} + y_{n} \\
y_{n+1} = bx_{n}
\end{cases}
$$

*classic henon attractor with parameters $a=1.4$ and $b=0.3$*
