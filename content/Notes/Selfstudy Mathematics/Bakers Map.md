from [[Dynamical Systems Theory]], a Bakers map is a [[Chaos|chaotic]] map from unit square onto itself. Named after the kneading operation you apply to make it

![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8c/Baker%27s_map_mixing.gif)

## Definition

$$
S_{baker-folded}(x,y) = \begin{cases}
\left( 2x, \frac{y}{2}\right)  & for \ 0 \leq x \leq \frac{1}{2} \\
\left( 2-2x, 1- \frac{y}{2} \right) & for \ \frac{1}{2} \leq x \leq 1
\end{cases}
$$
