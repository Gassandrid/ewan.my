title: Video Editing With Code

I saw a [fireship](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=deg8bOoziaE) video on some software that could be used to **edit and create videos with code**. While I might not have the full time to do this, I think it's a cool idea to look into as I really appreciate the text based programmatic style of media creation, which I already use Mermaid(with obsidian) and [[Manim]] to do for different tasks.

In addition if language models can get even better than they already are you could leverage that to edit media at a quite efficient rate.

Even without that, as some who is a [[Typing]] enthusiast, I feel as though I could make full use of this the same way I do with Mermaid and whatnot.
