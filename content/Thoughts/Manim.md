Manim is a really useful tool that allows you to create animations and illustrations programmatically, which is a much nicer experience than doing it by hand in Blender.

Manim is mostly known by its creator, [[3Blue1Brown]], but is now maintained by the community as the [community edition.](https://github.com/3b1b/manim)

I would make and post Manim animations on this website, but I dont have the time or the patience, and [desmos](https://www.desmos.com/) works fine for what I do.
