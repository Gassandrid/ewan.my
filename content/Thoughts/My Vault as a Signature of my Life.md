It's quite obvious that I write in this vault all the time. In the last few months, especially this year, I have really committed to writing a lot for the sake of myself, and I love it.

But more than that, I like the idea that there is evidence of me living outside of my own thoughts. By writing down all the time, I hope that people could get a grasp of what my mind might have looked like when I was alive. Maybe someone besides me is reading this right now! That would be cool. If so, I hope you find my stuff interesting!

For this reason, I really want to commit to filling this vault to the fullest possible extent. Obsidian has opened up the possibility of not only making a "second brain" as some call it, but an actual record of my knowledge, thoughts, and everyday life events. 

---

The one thing I have to work more on to make this better is actually writing about things that are happening in my day-to-day life. Like what thing happened a certain day, did I go out with friends, or how I felt, etc. I want this to be a portal into my own mind, but that won't really happen unless Truly open up here, however corny that sounds :)
