I love typing. It's one of my big addictions. When I am being unproductive, you will often find me on [monkeytype](https://monkeytype.com/).

Despite this, it is a very useful skill for me to have, as someone in the field of computer science. So, in a way, it is somewhat productive for to practice

[[On Touch Typing]] does a great job as describing *how* typing has affected my workflow.

---

## Setup

I alternate between using my [moonlander](https://www.zsa.io/moonlander) and my standard MacBook Pro 16 Keyboard. I am much faster on my MacBook Pro kebyoard, but I am slowly getting better with the moonlander, and I find it more reliable.

Typing has also led to me adopting ( as all computer scientists eventually do ) Neovim and Vim motions for everything I do. For those not familiar, Vim and Vim motions allow you to do everything on you keyboard( namely text editing ) with only your keyboard, and at a much faster speed than anything with a mouse.
