This image is a work of art by American contemporary artist Kara Walker.  The work is titled _The Hero with 1000 Diagnosable Disorders_.  It was created in 2024.  The medium used is watercolor and sumi-e ink on cut paper on paper.  Walker is known for using her art to explore themes of race, gender, sexuality, and violence, often referencing the history of American slavery and racism.
