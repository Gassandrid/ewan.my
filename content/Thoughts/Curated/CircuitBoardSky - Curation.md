This image is a two-color aquatint etching with hardground and drypoint titled **Circuit Board** by artist Analia Saban.  It measures 39 3/4 x 30 3/4" (101 x 78.1 cm). The work was created in 2021. It is a mixed-media artwork that combines technical diagrams with a sky motif. The edition size is 32.
