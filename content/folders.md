**Folders**! We love them because they are simple and **everyone** knows how they work. Because of that I don't see much point coming here as you can probably figure it out(and there isn't much to show here, although the sub folders have their own corresponding index pages.)

[[Notes/index|Notes]] is the most structured folder I have, it consists of my class notes and my self study notes by topic. They are not so much organic thoughts as they are pre-planned before I finish a class or a topic.

[[Projects/index|Projects]] is pretty self explanatory. Important to note that while these are publicly available, they are not written for other people, and are usually just a cluster of thoughts and ideas to help me track my work.

[[Thoughts/index|Thoughts]] is the big "[[Digital Garden]]" one. This is meant to be something like a "zettelkasten" notebook, which means single notes for single ideas, with little to no initial organization. This is where I hope the idea of **emergent organization** will really take root, but I am still working on the process.
